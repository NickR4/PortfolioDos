Components

SCHEDULE IT

First I will create an app for scheduleing a date and time to meet. Which includes a way to send the invitation and an RSVP system for invitees.

GROUP IT

Next I will create an app for groups. People can create, join, and leave groups. The invitations will be sent to all group members, and group members can RSVP creating a live list of who plans to attend.

MAP IT

Then I will create a map app which sets a marker of where to meet. This should be adjustable and visible to everyone. But it can only be set or modified by the designated leader.

COMBINE IT

Finally, I will combine all three components into one app! So there are groups who can schedule a day, time, and place to meet for a walk together! They can rsvp, but even if they don't they can still show up!""
